# Summary

Compact planetary gearbox for the NEMA17 stepper motor

external gear teeth 27
4 internal gears 9 teeth

gear ratio is 4:1

the output shaft bolt M5
M3 screws and nuts for mounting

Boxing thickness v1 - 12mm, v2 - 15mm

All parts are separated
your slicer successfully divide into separate parts
